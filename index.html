About me

Hi! I am Laura Pamela Aranda Medrano, a young Mexican leader deeply in love with life and driven by a relentless spirit of activism. My journey began in a way that most would find unusual: while other children were watching cartoons, I spent my mornings captivated by UN sessions on the screen as I got ready for kindergarten. That early spark evolved into a lifelong mission to redefine the complex heartbeat of our world through the lenses of Geopolitics, Public International Law, and the emerging frontier of Cosmic Law. I don't just study the world as it is; I work to build the world as it must become.

I am currently the driving force behind the ReImagine the UN movement, a global effort that uses a massive, inclusive survey to co-create a governance model fit for the challenges of our time—from the rapid rise of Artificial Intelligence to the urgent reality of climate change. Parallel to this, I am developing a specialized software designed to analyze the delicate balance of nations. My goal is to shift our global paradigm from a reactive stance to a preventive system, ensuring that peace and security are maintained by foresight rather than managed through crisis.

My ultimate ambition is to transform the very essence of geopolitics. I believe that global strategy must transcend the mere pursuit of power; it must be rooted in mental health, justice, and a profound planetary ethics. When I am not redesigning global systems, you will find me advocating for animals, fueled by the same passion that drives my activism. I am here to prove that through empathy and innovation, we can create a future that isn't just functional, but deeply human and ethically grounded for every inhabitant of this planet.

Projects

RE:UN Reimagine the United Nations

The current international security architecture, established in 1945, faces an unprecedented crisis of legitimacy. My research proposal, RE:UN, advocates for a structural evolution of the United Nations to transition from a power-centric hierarchy to a rules-based global order.

The reform focuses on four strategic pillars: Financial Independence through global levies, the Conditional Veto to empower the General Assembly, mandatory ICJ Integration, and a pragmatic Article 108 Strategy led by middle-power coalitions.

Your Voice Matters

Global Balance Index

Traditional International Relations theories focus on dominance; the Global Balance Monitor focuses on equilibrium. This project introduces The Balance Core, a tetradimensional framework that redefines power as the capacity to sustain proportional alignment across four foundational dimensions: Structure, Identity, Connectivity, and Planetary Ethics.
